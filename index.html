Tech Education Isn’t a Privilege
It’s a Necessity

At TechDiva Foundation, we believe tech education should be accessible to every student. We are dedicated to bridging the digital divide by providing hands-on coding and digital skills training to underserved communities.

Technology is shaping the future, yet millions of young girls, women, and underserved communities lack access to the education and skills needed to thrive in a digital world.

Bridging the Digital
Learning Gap

For many students, access to tech education remains a challenge. Whether due to limited resources, lack of exposure, or guidance, young girls and women are often excluded from opportunities in STEM. TechDiva Foundation is changing this by providing free tech education, coding programs, and digital skills training.

Programs & Initiatives

Empowering Girls, Women & Underserved Communities

All Girls Hackathon

A movement dedicated to inspiring young girls from government schools, orphanages, and underserved communities to take their first steps in technology. AGH provides mentorship, hands-on coding experience, and an inclusive space for first-time coders.

TechDiva Class

Bringing digital literacy and coding education to students from underserved communities through in-person and remote learning. We offer summer and regular classes, integrated within school hours or weekends, ensuring long-term skill-building and growth through hands-on digital learning.

Techies Without Borders

A volunteer-driven initiative connecting individuals, university students, and corporate professionals with meaningful opportunities to mentor and support students in digital education. Volunteers contribute through mentorship, content creation, social media outreach, and technical support.

TechHub

A community-based learning space providing free access to technology, coding, and digital skills training for women, young adults, and students from underserved communities. TechHub offers workshops, mentorship, and a collaborative environment to foster digital learning and career growth.
