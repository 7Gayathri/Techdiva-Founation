Community ComputerCodeLabs

A Space for Learning Innovation & Digital Growth

TechHub is a community-driven tech learning center designed to provide women, young adults, and children from underserved communities with access to digital education and skill development. It serves as a safe and inclusive space where individuals from diverse backgrounds and abilities can explore technology, coding, and digital literacy

Why TechHub Matters

Many individuals, particularly from underserved communities, lack access to tech education and structured digital skills training. TechHub was created to bridge this gap by offering free, hands-on learning opportunities in a supportive and inclusive environment

The TechHub Experience

At TechHub, every learner has the opportunity to explore, create, and innovate, ensuring that digital literacy and tech education are accessible to all. We focus on fostering an inclusive space where individuals can develop essential skills in a supportive environment

Hands-On Learning

Practical, experience-based sessions that equip learners with real-world digital skills they can apply immediately

Mentorship & Guidance

Support from tech professionals, educators, and volunteers, ensuring personalized learning experiences

Community-Centered Learning

A collaborative environment where individuals from diverse backgrounds come together to share knowledge, grow, and support one another

AGH Edition 1 Laying the Foundation

The first edition, held in Chennai, provided an entry point into tech for girls from diverse backgrounds, establishing a model that would drive future impact

  • 500+ participants from high schools, colleges, and non-CS backgrounds
  • 80% of participants expressed interest in pursuing STEM careers
  • Launched a mentorship model, enabling past participants to support new learners

TechHub Locations

TechHub Chennai

Located in Adyar, Chennai, in collaboration with HashHackCode, TechHub Chennai offers access to 15 systems for learning and development.

TechHub Trichy

Located in Kajamalai, Trichy, in collaboration with KLA Trust, TechHub Trichy offers 25 systems to support tech education for the local community.

TechHub Bangalore

Located in Kalkunte, Rural Bengaluru, in collaboration with Anjana Vidya Kendra, TechHub Bangalore offers access to 30 systems for rural community development
