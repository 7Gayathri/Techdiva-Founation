Empowering Students with Digital & Coding Skills

Building a Digital Learning Ecosystem

TechDiva Classes is an initiative that partners with schools and community centers to equip students from underserved communities with essential digital literacy and coding skills. It builds confidence, creativity, and problem-solving abilities, offering structured exposure to technology for a future-ready generation

Why TechDiva Classes Matters

Many students from low-income communities, struggle with fundamental digital skills due to limited access to computers and structured technology education. TechDiva Classes aims to bridge this digital learning gap by:

Introducing students to computers, coding, and digital workflows in a structured, hands-on learning environment

Offering both in-person and online mentorship, ensuring accessibility for students in various educational settings

Creating a sustainable learning model, where students progress from learners to mentors, driving long-term digital education within their communities.

The TechDiva Learning Experience

TechDiva Classes are designed with flexibility and support in mind, the program combines in-person and online mentorship with a progressive learning approach that introduces students to essential digital skills and gradually builds their confidence in advanced technology and coding

Flexible Scheduling

Sessions are held once or twice a week depending on student and school availability

Hybrid Learning Model

Students are guided by both in-person and online mentors, ensuring continuous support

Progressive Learning Approach

Students start with foundational digital skills before moving on to logic-based coding and creative programming

Many students entering TechDiva Classes through AGH begin with Summer Classes to build their confidence before transitioning to Regular Classes for continued learning.

Structured Learning with HashHackCode

TechDiva Classes follow the HashHackCode curriculum, offering a structured learning pathway that grows with each learner. From basic digital skills to logical thinking and creative coding, the program builds confidence through hands-on, progressive learning
