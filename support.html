Tech Education Isn’t a Privilege It’s a Necessity

TechDiva Foundation is dedicated to bridging the digital divide and empowering young girls, women, and underserved communities through tech education. Whether through volunteering, partnerships, or donations, your support creates real opportunities for those who need it most

Why TechDiva Classes Matters

Many students from low-income communities, struggle with fundamental digital skills due to limited access to computers and structured technology education. TechDiva Classes aims to bridge this digital learning gap by:

Introducing students to computers, coding, and digital workflows in a structured, hands-on learning environment

Offering both in-person and online mentorship, ensuring accessibility for students in various educational settings

Creating a sustainable learning model, where students progress from learners to mentors, driving long-term digital education within their communities.

Bridging the Digital
Learning Gap

For many students, access to tech education remains a challenge. Whether due to limited resources, lack of exposure, or guidance, young girls and women are often excluded from opportunities in STEM. TechDiva Foundation is changing this by providing free tech education, coding programs, and digital skills training.
